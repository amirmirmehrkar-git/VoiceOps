# VoiceOps Demo-to-Sale Call Script (20 minutes)

## Pre-Call (2 min)
- Review prospect's company/role
- Check their current tools (Jira? PagerDuty?)
- Prepare relevant demo scenario

## Opening (2 min)
"Thanks for your time. I know you're busy, so let's make this valuable.

**Quick context**: VoiceOps converts voice incident reports into production-ready tickets in 90 seconds.

**Today's agenda**:
1. Quick demo (5 min)
2. Your use case (5 min)
3. Pricing & next steps (5 min)
4. Q&A (3 min)

Sound good?"

---

## Demo (5 min)

### Step 1: Show the Problem (1 min)
"Right now, when someone reports an incident, they probably:
- Fill out a form (5-10 minutes)
- Wait for ticket creation
- Hope nothing was missed

**The cost**: Slow response, potential errors, manual work."

### Step 2: Show VoiceOps (3 min)
"Watch this:

[Live demo or video]
- Voice input
- Real-time processing
- JSON output
- Jira ticket created

**The result**: 90 seconds, 100% accurate, zero manual work."

### Step 3: Highlight Value (1 min)
"**What this means for you**:
- 10x faster incident reporting
- Zero data entry errors
- Your team can report while fixing the issue
- Automatic ticket creation"

---

## Discovery (5 min)

### Questions
1. "How many incidents do you handle per month?"
2. "What's your current process?"
3. "What's the biggest pain point?"
4. "How long does ticket creation take now?"
5. "What tools do you use? (Jira? PagerDuty?)"

### Listen for:
- Pain points
- Current costs
- Decision makers
- Timeline
- Budget

---

## Pricing & Next Steps (5 min)

### Present Pricing
"Based on what you've shared, I'd recommend [Plan].

**Pricing**: $X/month
**ROI**: You'll save [Y] hours/month = $Z in value

**Next step**: Free 2-3 week pilot. No commitment, no payment. You'll get:
- Full access
- Up to 100 incidents
- Integration setup
- Support

After the pilot, you'll have real data to decide."

### Handle Objections
See `objection_handling.md`

---

## Close (3 min)

### Trial Close
"Does this sound like it could help your team?"

### If Yes:
"Great! Let's get you set up:
1. I'll send pilot terms (non-binding)
2. We'll schedule setup call
3. You'll be live in 24 hours

When can you start?"

### If Hesitant:
"What concerns do you have? Let's address them."

### If No:
"Understood. What would need to change for this to work? Or is there a better time?"

---

## Follow-up

### If Closed:
- Send pilot terms
- Schedule setup call
- Send welcome email

### If Not Closed:
- Send follow-up email with:
  - Demo recording
  - ROI calculator
  - Case study
- Schedule follow-up call

---

## Key Metrics to Track

- Demo completion rate
- Pilot conversion rate
- Time to close
- Objections encountered
