## 1. Purpose

This DPA governs data processing for VoiceOps services.

- Voice recordings (transcripts)

- Voice-to-text conversion

- Webhook delivery

- Encryption at rest (AES-256)
- Access controls
- Audit logging

- Deletion: Upon request or contract termination
